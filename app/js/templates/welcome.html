Porysearch

Porysearch is a website with searchable information on Pokemon, moves, and abilities!

Searching

There are filters that may be used to search the Pokemon and moves described below:

Syntax Effect Example
type:<Type> Restricts the search to pokemon of a specific type. type:Water
