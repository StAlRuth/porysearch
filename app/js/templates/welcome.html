Porysearch

Porysearch is a website with searchable information on Pokemon, moves, and abilities!

Caught Pokemon

Click on the pokeball icon next to a pokemon to mark it as caught. It's good to keep track of your Pokedex progress!

To unmark a Pokemon as caught, just click on the greyed-out Pokeball icon next to its name on either the Caught Pokemon or Pokemon menu.

Searching

There are filters that may be used to search the Pokemon and moves described below:

Syntax Effect Example (Click!)
type:<Type> Restricts the search to pokemon of a specific type. type:Water
type:Grass
type:Fire
